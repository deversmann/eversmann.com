Wedding Party : Groomsman - Chris Armstrong
Chris and Damien both come from similar 'computer geek' backgrounds, having both received a degree from the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at the University of Toledo. Damien can't exactly remember when they met, but he's sure they hit it off from the start. Chris has an outgoing (almost goofy) persona that often provided the Costello to Damien's Abbott. Chris also seems to have a preparedness for any of life's events rarely seen among normal people (not that Chris is normal or anything). Whenever Damien had a problem, issue, difficulty or question, Chris was up to the challenge. This applied everywhere, even down to helping Damien with his Engagement Ring buying research.
