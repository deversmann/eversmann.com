Wedding Story : Amanda's Story
Upon graduating Amanda decided she wanted to major in music education at Capital University... and after a year and half, decided music wasn't a life long career for her. Amanda transferred to the University of Toledo as a psychology major. Her junior year marked the year she began working in residence life as an R.A. She had such a marvelous experience that she decided for one more year... but this time in a different building!
