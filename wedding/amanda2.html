Wedding Story : Amanda's Story
Amanda spent her academic career as a child at St. Mary's Central Catholic. It was high school that began the real adventure for Amanda... Her freshman year she got involved in the high school musical, "Working," where she had the privilege of meeting her now best friends. Her new found friends convinced her to join marching band (Amanda hadn't picked up a musical instrument in 4 years)... and the following year Amanda was marching with her trombone. From proms, black socks, Mindy and the F.G.P. to marching band, musicals, building tents and getting her tonsils removed... high school had some great memories.
