Wedding Party : Celebrant - Fr. Mark Davis
Father Mark and Amanda met when Father was the priest at Amanda's high school. Their relationship grew over a trip to Walt Disney World in Florida when Amanda asked Father Mark to "make it God" (in reference to the dinner rolls at the table). Priest is the best magician (Worlds 3rd tallest!!!) Amanda knows (not that she knows many). He has been one of the greatest influences on Amanda over the years. When it came down to picking a date for the wedding, the most important thing for Damien and Amanda to consider was if Father Mark was available.
