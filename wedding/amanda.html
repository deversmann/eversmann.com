Wedding Story : Amanda's Story
Amanda has always been all about giving... it is for this reason that she waited a whole two weeks to be born on March 15th, 1980 - her dad's birthday (what a great present!). Amanda grew up as a princess... I mean, only child. Despite living like an only child, she has two half sisters and one half brother... this situation took Amanda a while to comprehend- but she eventually realized who was really her mom.
