Wedding Info : Maps & Directions : Directions to Sandusky
From Toledo and points West: Take the Ohio Turnpike (I-80/90) East to exit 110/6A (OH-4). Turn left on Columbus-Sandusky Rd (OH-4) which will take you directly into Sandusky.
From Cleveland and points East: Take I-90/OH-2 West. Continue on OH-2 where it and I-90 split. Continue on to US-6/OH-2. US-6 will take you into Sandusky.
From Columbus and points South: Take I-71 North to exit 121 (Polaris Pkwy). Turn left on Polaris. Bear left onto E. Powell Rd. Turn right onto US-23 North (Columbus Pike). Follow US-23 to Marion-Bucyrus Rd and turn right (OH-4 North). OH-4 will take you directly into Sandusky.
