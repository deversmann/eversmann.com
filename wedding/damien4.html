Wedding Story : Damien's Story
Settling into a career didn't stop the winds of change for Damien though. After meeting Amanda through a mutual friend, Damien began to muse about owning a house. The change from renting to owning seemed imminent, until Amanda went to school at USF. So, always up for a change, Damien followed her to San Francisco until.... Well, a wedding is an expensive event (and so is living in Frisco), so Damien changed locales again, and relocated to Cincinnati to save money for the big event.
