Wedding Story : Damien's Story
When he moved on to high school at St. Xavier, Damien began to see the many options that were open to him (it seemed he could change his mind forever). What would he do? Well, the endless possibilities had him waiting till the last minute to choose the University of Toledo to study Business... till he got there. Next came Theatre... then Math and Physics... followed by Computer Science and Engineering... English... and finally Theatre. Not wanting to stay at one school too long, Damien gave up changing majors to get a degree in Theatre, and moved on to changing schools again. After one year in the graduate program in Theatre at Indiana University, Damien decided he had been in school long enough and it was time for another change.
